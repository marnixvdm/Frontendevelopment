Maesons, The ultimate men-experience

At MAESONS you’re at home. Relax, drink, play, get a haircut and get your feet done. Experience the treatments and space like you are at home. Don’t be shy and play a game with your friends, have a drink, Acai bowl or smoothie while you wait for your treatment. You will feel like a newborn man, ready to conquer the world!

MAESONS is located at the Voorstraat 21, near Neude, in Utrecht (The Netherlands). See you soon!
